MadLibs!

 Instructions
  • Input your madlibs story title in the top box
  • Input your story in the box below it, wherever you want the AI to fill in words, put an asterix (*) followed by the number of words you want generated
  • For example, if I put "When suddenly, he *10. The end".
  • The output would be "When suddenly, he (10 words generated by the AI). The end"
  • Pick the theme of the AI (each theme was trained on documents relevant to it)
  • Press 'Create Story' to generate your story!
 Neural net details
  • Accuracy: 30% (at picking the correct next word in a sentence on relevant documents)
  • Type: Recurrent
  • Time to Train: ~2 hours x 4 themes
  • Number of Classes: 10 000 words
 Sources
  • Scifi Corpus
  • PBS News Hour
