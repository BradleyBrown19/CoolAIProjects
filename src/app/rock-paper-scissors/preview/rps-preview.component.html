Rock, paper, scissors!

    **Must enable webcam to play**

    Instructions

  • To start the game, press the 'Start Game' button (very well labelled) at the top of video.
  • At the bottom of the screen, the text will change to rock, then to paper, then scissors.
  • After scissors, it is time to make your move! (Make a fist for rock, peace sign for scissors or a flat hand for paper)
  • Make sure your hand is in good view of the camera so it can see your move clearly.
  • The computer will make a move at the same time and will keep a counter of wins!
